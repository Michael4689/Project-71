Our school name is TMA, arbreviation for Tustin Memorial Academy. Our school located in Tustin, California. Here, students learn the most important curicullums for learning all the subject that students need to learn.

On campus, our students can meet with friends for projects, have a little fun, and/or celebrate an important event. We believe that the campus area is for social activity (in person). Students talk and share recent news.

TMA is really on attendance and the lunch count. Students can order lunch for free with just a 4 number code to enter in the cafeteria. And, a mac book pro laptop is awarded every year to a random student who had been absent less that 3 times and who was never sick during the school year.

The school is well known for our sports programs and math olympiad programs. TMA won the 2008 soccer award, the 2009 basketball AND the 2009 math olympiad award. In 2013, the soccer award again. Also, in 2016, TMA won volleyball boys teams award, and in 2018, the girls volleyball teams award.
